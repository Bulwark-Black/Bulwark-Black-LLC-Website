<p>On December 27, 2023, threat actor “APTlord” announced on the dark web forum RAMP that they were selling source code and other information owned by marinetraffic[.]com, which was allegedly obtained by the theft of backup data from web-based Git repository GitLab. Marinetraffic[.]com is an open-source analytics platform that provides real-time information on maritime vessels and activity around the globe. Some of the information available includes:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The geographic positions of vessels (accuracy and update frequencies vary between vessels)</li>



<li>Vessel details (such as photographs, country of operation, speed, design, and other physical attributes)</li>



<li>Journey details (such as originating locations and destinations and expected arrival times)</li>



<li>Information relating to maritime infrastructure (such as port locations, capacity, traffic statistics, and weather conditions)</li>
</ul>
