<h1 class="wp-block-heading">TBHM Live &#8211; Course Info</h1>



<p>I am thrilled to introduce you to The Bug Hunter&#8217;s Methodology LIVE, my masterclass designed for aspiring and seasoned offensive security professionals, including web application security testers, red teamers, and bug bounty hunters.</p>



<p>The Bug Hunter&#8217;s Methodology (TBHM) is a two-day, paid, virtual training that aims to equip you with the latest tools, techniques, and strategies, plus provide a data-driven methodology on how and where to search for vulnerabilities that are currently common in the wild.</p>



<p>Unlike other courses, TBHM Live is not an A-Z or beginner-oriented course. True to the spirit of my public TBHM talks, my emphasis is on expert tips, time-saving tricks, practical Q&amp;As, automation strategies, vetted resources, and engagement via the dedicated community on Discord.</p>



<p><strong>Here are the details for the upcoming masterclasses:</strong></p>



<p>Class 1 for US Timezone: March 2nd-3rd , 10-6 MST</p>



<p>Class 2 for EU Timezone: March 9th-10th , 5am-1pm MST</p>



<p>Each module will be driven live, using real-time targets where possible. You&#8217;ll have access to all source material to refer back to after the training. Plus, a video recording of the class will be available for all participants shortly after the course concludes.</p>



<p>TBHM Live is also much more than just a course. I am dedicated to fostering a vibrant and supportive community for our learners. In keeping with this commitment, I will maintain a Discord channel for ongoing support, including resume guidance and job placement assistance.</p>



<p>Join us for TBHM Live and get ready to supercharge your skills, refine your strategies, and join an active community of like-minded professionals.</p>
