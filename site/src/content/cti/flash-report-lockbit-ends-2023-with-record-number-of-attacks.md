<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-key-findings">Key Findings</h2>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Ransomware &amp; Digital Extortion (R&amp;DE) threat collective LockBit conducted more attacks during Q4 2023 (October-December) than in any other quarter, despite their activity accounting for a significantly reduced proportion of the wider threat landscape’s activity.</li>



<li>It is almost certain that LockBit is benefitting from the December disruption of other R&amp;DE collectives—particularly through procuring affiliates from ALPHV and NoEscape.</li>



<li>A greater proportion of LockBit’s attacks were leveraged against the manufacturing and retail industries in Q4 2023, both of which are above the threat landscape average.</li>
</ul>
